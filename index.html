Clinical calculators

CHB HCC Risk Calculators

Estimate hepatocellular carcinoma risk in chronic hepatitis B using HBsAg-HCC and PAGE-B, with shared inputs, continuous calculation, and EMR-ready results.

Portal Hypertension Predictor

Estimate clinically significant and severe portal hypertension using the Vienna laboratory models, with optional liver stiffness and supporting clinical interpretation.

Liver Injury Backtracker

Estimate earlier AST, ALT, and LDH values from current measurements using configurable enzyme half-lives.

Interactive clinical education

Interpreting the Liver Panel

Work through evolving liver tests, injury patterns, differential diagnosis, and management decisions in acute and chronic liver injury.

Cirrhosis Interactive Case

A case-based approach to cirrhosis, decompensation, major complications, and transplant evaluation.

Physiologic simulators

Portal Pressure Simulator

Explore how portal flow, vascular resistance, TIPS, and portosystemic shunting alter portal pressure and HVPG.

Liver Simulator

Explore liver injury and treatment effects through an interactive model of changing laboratory trajectories.

Research & writing

Abstract Atelier

A PubMed-aware workspace for drafting and refining medical abstracts with integrated reference support.

HA Floorplan Editor

A visual editor for creating and exporting interactive Home Assistant floorplans.

About

Mohammad Almeqdadi, MD, trained in internal medicine at St. Elizabeth’s Medical Center, gastroenterology at SUNY Downstate, and transplant hepatology at Lahey Clinic. He is a transplant hepatologist and gastroenterologist at Tufts Medical Center in Boston. These tools grew out of clinical practice, trainee education, research, and a recurring need to make complex liver concepts easier to use.
