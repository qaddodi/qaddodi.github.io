Medicine gives me the questions. Technology gives me another way to investigate them.

I’m Mohammad Almeqdadi, MD—a physician and creative technologist interested in hepatology, clinical reasoning, medical education, simulation, and human-centered software.

These projects are small experiments in making complex systems more visible: tools you can manipulate, pathways you can trace, and ideas you can learn by exploring rather than memorizing.
