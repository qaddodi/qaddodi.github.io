Clinical calculators

CHB HCC Risk Calculators

Estimate hepatocellular carcinoma risk in chronic hepatitis B using HBsAg-HCC and PAGE-B, with shared inputs, continuous calculation, and EMR-ready results.

Guo et al., Aliment Pharmacol Ther 2026 · Papatheodoridis et al., J Hepatol 2016

Portal Hypertension Predictor

Estimate clinically significant and severe portal hypertension using the Vienna laboratory models, with optional liver stiffness and supporting clinical interpretation.

Reiniš et al., J Hepatol 2023 · Vienna 3P/5P models

Liver Injury Backtracker

Estimate earlier AST, ALT, and LDH values from current measurements using configurable enzyme half-lives.

Configurable elimination model · Educational estimate

About

I am a transplant hepatologist at Tufts Medical Center and an assistant professor at Tufts University School of Medicine, after fellowship training in gastroenterology at SUNY Downstate and transplant hepatology at Lahey. These tools grew from clinical practice, trainee education, and an interest in making complex liver physiology easier to explore.
